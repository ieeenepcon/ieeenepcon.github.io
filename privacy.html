Privacy Policy

How 2026 IEEE NEPCON collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: 8 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the 2026 IEEE NEPCON organizing committee, on behalf of the IEEE Nepal Section, handles personal information collected through this website and the conference registration and submission process.

1. Information we collect

  • Submission data: author names, affiliations, email addresses, and paper content submitted through the ConfConnects portal.
  • Registration data: name, affiliation, contact details, IEEE membership status, and attendance mode.
  • Payment data: transactions are processed by a third-party payment aggregator; we do not store full card details.
  • Website data: basic technical information such as browser type and pages visited, used to operate and improve the site.

2. How we use your information

  • To manage paper review, acceptance, publication, and inclusion in the proceedings and IEEE Xplore.
  • To process registration and issue certificates and conference materials.
  • To communicate with authors and attendees about the conference program and logistics.
  • To meet IEEE and legal, accounting, and reporting obligations.

3. Sharing your information

We share information only as needed to run the conference — for example, with IEEE (as technical co-sponsor and publisher), the review committee, and service providers such as the submission platform and payment aggregator. We do not sell your personal information. Where papers are published, author names, affiliations, and paper metadata become part of the public record.

4. IEEE privacy

As an IEEE-sponsored conference, processing associated with IEEE publication and membership is also governed by the IEEE Privacy Policy.

5. Data retention & security

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes above and to comply with legal obligations, and we apply reasonable safeguards to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to obligations that require us to retain certain records (for example, published papers). Contact us via the Contact page.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; the "last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.

This policy is a template for the organizers to review and adopt, including any local data-protection requirements, before publishing.
