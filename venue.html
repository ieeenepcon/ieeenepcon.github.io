About the City

A conference in the heart of the Himalayas

Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, blends centuries of heritage with a growing technology and research community. Delegates can experience UNESCO World Heritage sites, the vibrant old city, and easy access to the Himalayan foothills — all within a compact, welcoming city.

Travel note: Many nationalities can obtain a visa on arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport (KTM); please verify current requirements for your country before travelling. Contact the organizing committee for an official invitation letter if required for your visa application.
